### Color Palette System
The custom color palette system is based on professional pixel art conventions:
- Maria (player): Warm tones (pink dress, brown hair, skin tones)
- Enemies: Varied but cohesive (green slime, purple bat, white skeleton)
- Environment: Natural tones (greens for grass/trees, earth tones for buildings)
- UI: High contrast gold/red/blue for visibility
